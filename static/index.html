Drug detector
1
Turn on the device and identify the user on the screen
2
Collect saliva until the red circle appears and put it inside the compression tube
3
Push the blue septum on the Salliva Collector to fill both vials up to the line
4
Put the lid and turn it up and down three times to mix.
5
Press the specific Drug Vial and add 3 drops on hall (S) on the Drug Chip.
6
Wait 10 minutes for the incubation of the sample on the Drug Chips.
7
Add 1 drop of the Ampoulle Buffer onto hall (S) and (C).
8
Introduce the selected Drug Chips oriented as shown in the picture.
9
Select the drug on the screen, press measure and wait for the result.
